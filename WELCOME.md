## WELCOME ! ##

Overview

The goal is to teach you to code with R on data organization, visualization, & analyses. It's free and no coding background is required!

Over the course of 4 weeks tentatively from May 16 to June 13, we will meet for 2 hours total once a week every Saturday at 1pm (exact date and time are TBD pending room booking with the Psychology Department).

The learning objectives of this workshop are to help you develop a new skillset in R programming and a corresponding GitHub page link to add to your CV or resume, which will ultimately better prepare you for professional/graduate school or the workforce!

### The workshop schedule is as follows: ###

Weeks 1 - R + Github Setup: Installing and learning to familiarize yourself with R and Github \
Week 2 - Data Preprocessing: Learning to organize your datasets \
Week 3 - Data Analyses: Learning to conduct correlations and ANOVAs \
Week 4 - Data Visualization: Learning to graph your datasets \

Requirements: No coding background is required but please bring your own laptop and be motivated to learn and have fun :)
